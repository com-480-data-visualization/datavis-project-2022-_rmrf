Bird Distribution

_rmrf Team

This website enables one to exhaust statistics features of various birds around the world: how they distribute within different periods, what’s the variation between regions, what’s the abundance of population, etc.

Geometric Visualization

In this section, we will show the range of birds at different times of the year. You can select different filters in the drop-down boxes to get the desired results. The same holds true for the different time and space options, you can get information about different areas directly on the map. You can also get an animation of the distribution of birds over a certain time frame.

Statistic Features

In this part, we show explicit statistic features of different birds around worldwide. Chart plot shows basic statistics about the abundance and population of a bird within different regions, while pie chart gives a more intuitive view on the rank of bird distributions. Both plots provide exhuasted conclusions about bird distributions.
