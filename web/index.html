Bird Distribution

Team _rmrf

This website offers the visualization of statistics features about various bird species around the world: their distribution in different periods, the variation among different geographic regions, the population abundance, etc.

Geographic Visualization

In this section, we will show the range of birds at different times of the year.

You can select different filters in the drop-down boxes to get the desired results. The same holds true for the different time and space options, you can get information about different areas directly on the map.

You can also get an animation of the distribution of birds over a certain time frame.
